# 14 - Operational Runbooks - Azure Portal

## Objective

Create portal-oriented operational runbooks for destination services.

## Steps

1. Open Log Analytics and save useful queries.
2. Open Application Insights and review failures and dependencies.
3. Open Service Bus metrics and review queue length and dead-letter count.
4. Open APIM analytics for route-level failures.
5. Open Key Vault diagnostics for regulated access events.
6. Document rollback and escalation contacts.

## Expected Outcome

Operations teams know where to troubleshoot destination services in Azure.
