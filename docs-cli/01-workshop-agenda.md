# 01 - Workshop Agenda

## Objective

Understand the workshop sequence and choose the modernization track.

## Tracks

| Track | Target platform | Best for | Main services |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Track A: Simple | Azure Container Apps | Fast onboarding and small teams | Container Apps, Log Analytics, VNet |
| Track B: Enterprise | AKS + APIM + Service Bus | Platform teams and production patterns | AKS, ACR, APIM, Service Bus, App Insights |
| Track C: Regulated | Private networking + Key Vault + Defender | Financial, public sector, regulated workloads | Private VNets, Key Vault, private access, Defender |

## Flow

1. Validate prepared source environment.
2. Deploy destination VNet and platform for selected track.
3. Peer or connect source and destination VNets as needed.
4. Analyze eShopOnWeb bounded contexts.
5. Containerize and deploy extracted services.
6. Route traffic and add asynchronous messaging.
7. Add observability, security, and cost review.
8. Clean up destination resources.

## Expected Outcome

You know which track you are following and what you will build in the destination environment.
